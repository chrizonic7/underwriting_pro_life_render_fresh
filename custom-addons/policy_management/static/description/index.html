Policy Creation & Administration

Enables insurance companies to create, manage, and administer life insurance policies efficiently, including policyholder data capture and policy lifecycle tracking.

Policy Endorsements

Facilitates flexible modifications to existing policies such as changes to coverage, addition/removal of riders, and updates to policy terms.

Policy Renewals

Manages renewals for active, expired, or soon-to-expire policies with automatic notifications and status tracking dashboards.

Underwriting

Provides automated underwriting rules, risk profiling, and tariff administration for premium determination based on age, gender, and sum assured.

Policy Quoting

Generates customizable quotes with flexible deductibles and coverage levels, tracking quote-to-policy conversions.

Premium Collection & Receivables

Integrates with e-payment platforms for automated collections, payment tracking, reminders, and receivables reporting.

Reinsurance Management

Supports Facultative, Quota Share, and Excess of Loss programs with automated reinsurance allocation at underwriting.

Claims Management Suite

  • Claims Reporting: Via portal, email, USSD, or mobile app with guided intake and document upload.
  • Claims Processing: Automated workflow for validation, verification, and processing.
  • Decision Support: Access to policy data and claims history with business rule-based decisions.
  • Communication: Real-time notifications between stakeholders via email/SMS.
  • Settlement: Disbursement or denial of claims based on policy conditions.
  • Fraud Detection: AI-driven analysis for anomaly detection and red flagging.
  • Reinsurance Recovery: Automates recovery from reinsurers for all program types.
  • Analytics & Reporting: Interactive dashboards and exportable reports on claims trends and performance.
